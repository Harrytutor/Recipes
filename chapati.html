Making Chapati

Description

This is a common snack in East Africa and in India, where it is known as Roti. It is basically made up of Flour and can be eaten with any tea, stew or drink.

Ingredients

  • Flour: 2 cups
  • Eggs: 4
  • Water: 1 cup
  • Cooking oil: 1/2 a litre
  • Salt
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Mix flour, butter, eggs, salt, & sugar. Add water and then make a nice dough. Allow it to rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Cut your dough into small balls the size of your palm.
  3. Using a roller stick, make a ball circular and of desired size.
  4. Place the circular dough on a pre-heated pan. Allow it to dry and turn it on its other side. Add oil on the side on the pan and gently rotate as you wait for it to turn golden brown. Do the same on the other side.
  5. Repeat the same process for all the dough, and there you have your Chapati's.
