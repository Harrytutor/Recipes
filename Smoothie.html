Making the Perfect Smoothie

Description

This Pineapple-passion smoothie is an accompanying drink to our chapati snack and beef stew.

Ingredients

  • 1 sliced pineapple
  • 4 peeled passion fruits
  • 6 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place all the Ingredients in a blender and blend completely, until smooth.
  2. Serve in a glass and enjoy.
