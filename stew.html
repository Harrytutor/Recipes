Making Beef Stew

Description

This meat stew is the best to accompany our main snack; Chapati, and it takes about 30 minutes to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Beef meat
  • Vegetables: tomatoes, potatoes, corriander, onions, & garlic
  • Salt, cooking oil, black pepper
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil your meat on medium fire, and add salt, and black pepper.
  2. Later add all your Vegetables add stir gently to allow the mixture to be even, and allow it to cook fully.
  3. Serve your stew when it ready.
